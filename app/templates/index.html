Chilli Disease Detection

The Chilli Disease Detection is an innovative and user-friendly tool designed to assist farmers, agricultural experts, and enthusiasts in accuratelydiagnosing diseases affecting chilli plants. With the aim ofearlydetection and effective management, this web app leverages cutting-edge technologies to analyze images ofchilli plants and provide disease identification.
